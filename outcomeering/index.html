Outcomeering

The next management discipline.

Management helped organizations coordinate work. Analytics helped organizations understand work. Outcomeering helps organizations continuously improve outcomes.

Core ideaSystem first
  1. 01
    Current outcomes

    Every organization is perfectly designed to produce the outcomes it currently gets.

  2. 02
    System change

    If outcomes do not improve, the system must change.

  3. 03
    Discipline

    Outcomeering is the discipline of designing those systems.

Five principles

The operating beliefs of Outcomeering.

01Outcomes over activity

Measure results, not effort.

02Interventions over insights

Insights create awareness. Interventions create value.

03Learning over reporting

Organizations improve through learning, not reporting.

04Systems over heroics

Great outcomes emerge from great systems.

05Compounding over optimization

Build systems that improve continuously.

The Outcome Loop

Every Outcome Machine runs this loop.

LoopMonitor to learn
  1. 01
    Monitor

    Track outcome performance and leading signals.

  2. 02
    Explain

    Identify why outcomes changed.

  3. 03
    Recommend

    Choose the highest-leverage intervention.

  4. 04
    Execute

    Coordinate action through accountable owners.

  5. 05
    Learn

    Use the result to improve the next decision.

Outcome Twins

Living representations of how outcomes are produced.

Outcome Twins represent the entities, relationships, constraints, and signals that shape performance. They enable organizations to understand, predict, and improve performance continuously.
